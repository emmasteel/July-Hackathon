The process outlasts the tools

The tools we use will keep changing, fast. Much of what we covered today may look different in a year. What doesn't change — and what's worth getting good at — is the way we approach the work.

Before you go build

  • Capture what you learn. A mistake you'd rather not repeat, or a fix that worked, is a gift to your future self.
  • Skillify it. When an insight keeps coming back, turn it into an instruction, a prompt file, or a checklist that applies the lesson next time.
Creative problem-solving outlasts the tools, because what to build — and what good looks like — are still your decisions to make.

That's the "how". Now: open your team folder and start on C1. Go build. ▶
