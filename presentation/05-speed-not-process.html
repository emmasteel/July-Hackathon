The talk · the big idea

AI changed the speed, not the process

We still understand the problem and the people we're solving it for. We explore directions. We specify what good looks like. We plan the work. We build to the plan, apply standards, run tests, and validate the result. We learn, so next time we start from a stronger place.

All of it now with the assistance of AI — but we still make the decisions along the way.

Why that matters for you today

Copilot will type faster than you can. Your job isn't to out-type it — it's to decide what to build, judge whether the result is any good, and catch what it gets wrong.

The future favours creative problem-solvers who bring judgement and taste to build something people love.
