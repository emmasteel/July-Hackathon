Welcome & framing

Same objectives, two use cases

Both teams build an accessible, secure, public-facing government web feature. Only the domain differs.

  • Team 1 — ABR

    Accessible ABN lookup

    Analyse the Australian Business Register for accessibility gaps against WCAG 2.2 AA, then build an accessible ABN lookup/results feature that shows how to do it right.

  • Team 2 — Grant Finder

    Accessible grants checker

    Build an accessible grants / eligibility checker that helps a small business find grants it qualifies for — and explains why each one matches.

Ground rule: never call the live government services. Use the local fixtures only — your app and tests run on sample data.

Your track folder has a working skeleton, user stories, and tests to extend.
