# Specify — why and what, never *how*

When you have a direction worth building, turn it into a written contract. Hand the agent what you learned and ask it to write a spec *for you to review*. This is your **C2**.

## A spec covers

- **What we're building** — the deliverable, in plain language.
- **Why it matters** — the value, the reason for doing it now.
- **Acceptance criteria** — concrete, verifiable, observable. Not "looks good".
- **Out of scope** — what we're explicitly *not* doing.
- **Dependencies & open questions** — what we still need to resolve.

<div class="callout">
Pedantic beats vibe. A spec you can check beats a vibe you can't.
</div>

<p class="pixel">Tip: keep the spec in the repo (e.g. spec.md) so Copilot — and your team — can refer back to it.</p>
